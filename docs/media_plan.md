# Video + assets plan (verified July 2026)

The hackathon requires a **real screen recording of the deployed app**. Qwen
media models are decoration *around* that capture, never a replacement.

## Pipeline
1. **Script + captions** — `qwen-max` (OpenAI-compatible). ~pennies.
2. **Bilingual voiceover** — **`qwen3-tts-flash`** on `dashscope-intl.aliyuncs.com/api/v1`.
   `language_type: "Auto"` handles mixed zh+en in one pass. ~$0.05 for a 3-min
   script (free under the 1M-char/90-day quota). Output `.wav`.
3. **Title card + section dividers + thumbnail** — `qwen-image-2.0` ($0.035/img)
   or `wan2.2-t2i-flash` ($0.025/img). Flat price per image.
4. **Optional 3–5s intro sting** — `wan2.6-i2v` or `wan2.2-i2v-flash`: animate our
   logo / a hero screenshot. ~$0.50–0.80 for a 5s 1080p clip. New Singapore
   accounts get ~1,650 free video-seconds (90 days) — likely $0.
5. **Core demo footage** — REAL screen recording of
   https://mark24680617.github.io/quotepilot/ (OBS/QuickTime/Loom, or the
   browser-automation capture we already drive). AI video CANNOT show the real
   product — do not use Wan for product footage (reads as fake → DQ risk).
6. **Assembly / captions / VO sync** — ffmpeg or CapCut. No Qwen model edits video.

## API notes
- Video + TTS are **native DashScope async/sync calls**, NOT the OpenAI-compatible
  chat endpoint. Video = create-task → poll `GET /tasks/{id}` (~1–5 min); URLs
  expire 24h. TTS = sync HTTP (or WebSocket for realtime).
- Only text and `qwen-image` fit the OpenAI-compatible surface.
- Newest models (Wan 2.6/2.7, CosyVoice v3.x) live on workspace MaaS URLs
  `https://{WorkspaceId}.ap-southeast-1.maas.aliyuncs.com/...`; the classic
  `dashscope-intl.aliyuncs.com/api/v1` still works for `qwen3-tts-flash`.

## Budget
Essentially $0 with free quotas; a couple of dollars worst case. Only long 1080p
video clips burn money (10s ≈ $1.50) — cap AI video at a short intro/outro.

## Architecture diagram
Do it as **diagram-as-code** (Mermaid or SVG) generated by `qwen-max`/`qwen3-coder-plus`,
rendered to PNG. NOT an image model (garbles text labels). Clean, precise, editable.
